Sentiment Classification Model

Summary: An LSTM-based sentiment classifier trained on 100,000 tweets, reaching 90% accuracy and F1 = 0.88.

Expanded Description:
This model integrates a complete NLP pipeline: data collection, tokenization, word embeddings, training, and real-time inference with caching. Deployed in a chatbot, it adapts tone and responses dynamically, enabling conversations that are empathetic, engaging, and context-aware.

Applications: Chatbots, customer support, opinion mining, and adaptive conversational agents.

Stack: Python, TensorFlow/PyTorch, LSTM, NLP Pipeline, Docker.

Status: Validated prototype. Next steps include transformer-based improvements (BERT, GPT fine-tuning) and multilingual extension.
